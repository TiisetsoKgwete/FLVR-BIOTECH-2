Food Security

The system is failing across four linked fronts.

  1. Food shortage: Climate shocks, yield instability, and constrained land expansion are widening the gap between demand and supply.
  2. Food wastage: Large portions of global production are lost after harvest or wasted after distribution, even while hunger persists.
  3. Agricultural pollution: High-emission production models intensify energy use, water stress, and climate-linked instability across supply chains.
  4. Animal-system pressure: Industrial livestock systems magnify welfare concerns, resource inefficiency, and antibiotic dependency at scale.

Six Pillars of Food Security

  • Access

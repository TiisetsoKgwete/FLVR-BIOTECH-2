The Challenge

Four crises. One mission.

Food Shortage

By 2050 we need to produce 70% more food with only 4% more arable land, while climate change strips 20% of current yield.

Animal Cruelty

Over 80 billion land animals are raised for food every year under industrial conditions. Alternative proteins offer a humane path forward.

Agricultural Pollution

Agriculture accounts for 30% of global energy use, 14.5% of greenhouse gas emissions, and massive antibiotic overuse in livestock.

Food Wastage

1.3 billion metric tons of food wasted annually. 14% lost between harvest and retail.
